Want to Join?


We are looking for students who aren't afraid of tackling difficult problems and pushing their creativity, technical knowledge, and eagerness to learn. We have three sub-teams that provide different opportunities.

DESIGN
Focuses on the design and propulsion system of the aircraft. Responsible for creating parts in SolidWorks, running simulations, material selection, and propulsion system.

ELECTRONICS
Focuses on the electronics and software aboard the aircraft. Responsible for battery selection, flight controller, wiring layout, and necessary software.

OPERATIONS
Focuses on the business and management of the club. Responsible for sponsorship acquisition, fund management, recruiting, and club oversight. 
*We are currently looking for a Treasurer to join our Executive Team, no experience required*

Even though the club is primarily engineering, we are open to all majors regardless of engineering knowledge or experience! We also encourage our members to be apart of multiple sub-teams to learn new skills and viewpoints.
